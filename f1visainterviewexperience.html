Everything you need to know about F1 Visa

The first step for F1 visa application is to get the I-20 form from the university you have received an admit from. The I-20 is a comprehensive document containing your admit information, annual cost of attendance, funds info, and SEVIS ID. A sample I-20 form is shown below.

After receiving you I-20, you may pay the SEVIS (Student and Exchange Visitor Information System) fee ($350) with the SEVIS id mentioned in your I-20 here -> SEVIS / I-901 portal After payment, the system will generate a receipt and keep it safely.

Then comes your non-immigrant Visa application aka DS-160. Start it here -> DS-160. DS-160 is a long form to be filled online, fill it carefully and don't lie there. After you submit your DS-160 application a conformation page will come up. Get it printed and keep it safe. Hack: If you are not sure where (eg. Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai etc.) to submit the application, then you may just start your application and get the confirmation number. This number can be used to book Visa appointment even though the application is incomplete. After booking an appointment, you can complete and submit the DS-160 application to the respective city where you got Visa interview appointment. A sample DS-160 confirmation page is shown below.

It's time to get Visa appointments. Create an account & login here -> cgifederal.secure.force.com.

There are two stages of Visa appointements:

  • OFC / VAC Appointement (Where Biometrics, Photograph are taken and DS-160 confirmation page is stamped)
  • Consular Appointment (Where a Consular Officer takes interview to approve your Visa)

First step is to pay the Visa MRV fee ($165). Once you login and start the process by entering your details, you'll be promted to make payment. There are options to pay like NEFT, IMPS etc. NEFT from netbanking worked fine in my case. After you make the paymennt, it may take several hours to get reflected in your account. Once that is reflected, you will be able to look for appointment dates. Even if OFC dates are available, your appointment will not be confirmed unless both the types are available. Keep looking for it.
*Don't log in more than thrice to check available dates. Otherwise you'll be blocked for 72 hours. Ideally try twice in different time of the day. The best way is to join Telegram, Whatsapp groups where updates on availability of appointments are available.
